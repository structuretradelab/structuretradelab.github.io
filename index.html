✅ The Pre-Trading Checklist

6 rules I follow after 6 years

✓ Start with a demo account — no exceptions

2-3 months minimum. Prove you can be profitable before risking real money.

✓ Risk 1-2% per trade maximum

This is the golden rule. Break it and you will blow your account eventually.

✓ Low leverage saves lives

1:10 or 1:20 for beginners. Ignore brokers offering 1:1000 — that's how they profit from your losses.

✓ Have a trading plan before you enter

Entry, stop loss, take profit, position size. Write it down. Stick to it.

✓ Journal every trade

You can't improve what you don't measure. Track your wins and losses.

✓ Stop loss = survival. Always use it.

Every trade. No exceptions. Even when you're "sure" it will reverse.
