My Story — Why I Built This

I started trading forex in 2019 with absolutely no idea what I was doing. I had watched a few YouTube videos, saw someone flashing a rented Lamborghini, and thought, "I can do that." Spoiler alert: I couldn't.

My first year was a disaster. I blew up two accounts within months. I was over-leveraging, revenge trading, chasing losses, and trusting "gurus" who promised guaranteed profits. I lost thousands of dollars before I realized that everything I thought I knew was wrong.

So I started over. I went back to demo trading. I read every reputable book I could find. I studied market structure, risk management, and trading psychology. I journaled every single trade for two years. Slowly, painfully, I started to get better.

By year three, I stopped losing money. By year four, I became consistently profitable. Now, after six years of screen time and tens of thousands of trades, I've developed a systematic approach based on market structure and strict risk management.

Structure Trade Lab is where I share what actually works. No courses. No get-rich-quick schemes. No paid signals. Just honest education from someone who learned the hard way — so you don't have to.

My Trading Philosophy

  • 📐 Trade with structure, not emotion — Every trade follows a plan, never a feeling.
  • 🛡️ Risk management > everything — You can have a mediocre strategy and survive with good risk management.
  • 📊 Less is more — Master 1-2 pairs instead of watching 20 poorly.
  • 🧠 Psychology is 80% of the game — Strategy doesn't matter if you can't control fear and greed.
  • 📝 Journal or it didn't happen — You can't improve what you don't measure.
  • ⏳ Patience pays — The best trades are the ones you don't take.
